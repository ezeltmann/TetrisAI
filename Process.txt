# of NN Players (assuming 200 to start)
    Put players with Games of Tetris (Headless one)
        Have them play
        Until They Lose Some Number of times (1 or more)
        Take the best 20%
        Create a new generation
        Start again

    Take the best NN and write it to a file (that can be loaded again)

Use the NN file to play tetris on its own (with graphics)
